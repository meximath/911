Need emergency assistance?

If you need emergency assistance, dial 911 immediately. Emergency assistance is not available through the National 911 Program. The 911.gov website is maintained by the National 911 Program, which is housed within the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration's Office of Emergency Medical Services.
